General Information:

  1. The total duration of the examination is 190 Minutes.

Navigating to a Question:

To answer a question, do the following:

  • Click on the question number in the Question Palette on the right of your screen to go to that question directly.
  • Click on Save & Next to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.
  • Click on Mark for Review & Next to save your answer for the current question, mark it for review, and then go to the next question.

Question Status Legend:

1 You have not visited the question yet.
2 You have visited the question but not answered it.
3 You have answered the question.
4 You have NOT answered the question, but have marked it for review.
5 The question is answered and marked for review (it will be considered for evaluation).
